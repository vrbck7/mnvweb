As part of providing the services of the Monvee application, we process the following categories of personal data:

3. Why We Collect Personal Data – Legal Basis

We process personal data exclusively on valid legal bases within the meaning of Article 6 of the GDPR:

  • Performance of a Contract (Art. 6(1)(b) GDPR): We process registration data and financial records in order to provide you with the service you requested by registering for the application.
  • Compliance with a Legal Obligation (Art. 6(1)(c) GDPR): In the case of paid services, we process your invoicing data for the purpose of fulfilling tax and accounting obligations (e.g., the Accounting Act).
  • Legitimate Interest (Art. 6(1)(f) GDPR): We process technical data (logs) for the purpose of ensuring the security of the application, system stability, and error detection.
  • Consent (Art. 6(1)(a) GDPR): In the case of sending marketing communications, we process your e-mail address exclusively on the basis of your prior consent, which you can revoke at any time.

4. How We Use Personal Data

We use the personal data of users exclusively for the purposes for which it was collected:
